Our Expertise

FMCW Technology

Our radar employs Frequency Modulated Continuous Wave (FMCW) technology, which provides superior range resolution and accuracy. This allows for precise detection and tracking of even the smallest drones and other airborne threats.

Ku-band Operation

Operating in the Ku-band frequency range, our radar system offers high resolution and improved target detection capabilities. This frequency band is ideal for identifying and tracking small, low-flying objects that might evade other detection systems.

Polarimetric Electronically Steered Antennas

Polarimetric electronically steered antennas enable rapid scanning, enhancing target differentiation and distinguishing drones from other objects for improved coverage.

Enhanced Surveillance

Designed specifically for border surveillance, our radar system provides continuous monitoring and real-time threat assessment. This ensures prompt detection and response to unauthorized intrusions or potential security threats.

Robust and Reliable
